Support

BayAfya help and support

Use the guidance below to find the right path for account help, role access, patient workflows, and hospital operations support.

1Account and sign-in issues
If you cannot sign in, confirm you are using the right BayAfya role profile. Staff access depends on a hospital-issued authorization code. Patients may self-register or use an approved hospital invitation path.
2Hospital access or role mismatch
If your role or hospital workspace looks wrong, contact your hospital owner or admin. They can review hospital access, invitation history, and role assignment without creating duplicate user identities.
3Walk-in, triage, and admission support
Reception should capture walk-ins, nurses should complete triage, doctors should document consultation and downstream orders, and admissions teams should continue bed placement using the linked patient context.
4Laboratory and pharmacy workflow support
Lab technicians should work from clinician-requested investigations and return structured results for review. Pharmacists should work from active dispensing tasks or approved prescription-linked instructions.
5Patient records, privacy, and approvals
If a patient-specific record cannot be opened, the user may be outside their allowed access scope. Use active encounters, patient approval, or hospital-admin approval paths instead of bypassing privacy controls.
6Assistant and AI support
BayAfya Assistant can help with symptoms, mental wellbeing, workflow guidance, and permitted chart context. If AI output appears off, rely on clinical review and continue with the documented care path.
