BayAfya terms and conditions

Use of BayAfya is governed by hospital-approved access, patient privacy rules, clinical governance, and responsible operational conduct.

1Authorized access and identity responsibility
Only approved users may access BayAfya within their assigned role and hospital scope. Users must keep their credentials secure, use the correct profile type, and avoid sharing accounts or bypassing hospital approval paths.
2Clinical use and professional judgment
BayAfya supports care coordination, documentation, and workflow management. It does not replace clinician judgment, escalation policy, or formal hospital procedure. AI-supported outputs must be reviewed in context before action.
3Patient record handling
Patient records must be accessed only for approved care, operational, or governance purposes. Unauthorized browsing, copying, or disclosure of patient information is prohibited and may trigger audit review.
4Hospital workflow integrity
Reception, triage, consultation, laboratory, pharmacy, surgery, and admissions workflows should be recorded in BayAfya as accurately and promptly as possible so teams remain aligned and the patient journey is not fragmented.
5Cross-hospital participation
A single user identity may operate across multiple hospitals where access is approved. Cross-hospital data visibility remains limited to approved scope, patient consent paths, and hospital governance rules.
6Platform misuse and enforcement
BayAfya may restrict or revoke access for misuse, unsafe behavior, policy violations, or security concerns. Hospital leadership may review audit trails and activity records where necessary.
